## The Problem

The US accounting industry is facing a structural workforce crisis:

- **75% of CPAs** are approaching retirement age, with no equivalent pipeline of replacements
- **33% decline** in CPA exam candidates since 2016
- Partners -- the most expensive resource in a firm -- spend **50% of their day** answering questions their team should already know
- When a senior employee leaves, their knowledge walks out the door

Firms are solving this today with tribal knowledge, interruptions, and overtime. Those solutions don't scale, and the talent gap is only widening.

## The Solution

**A digital twin for professional service firms.** Our AI platform connects to a firm's existing systems, builds a unified intelligence layer, and lets anyone ask questions in plain English.

- Junior staff ask the AI before interrupting a partner
- New hires get answers on day one -- from the firm's own documented procedures
- Financial questions are answered in seconds, with cited sources from real data
- The firm gets smarter every day as the system accumulates operational knowledge

We don't replace QuickBooks, Xero, or any system of record. We connect to all of them and make the data intelligent.
