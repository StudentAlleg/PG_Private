<td valign="top" rowspan="2" width="20%">

### Value Propositions

**1. Operational intelligence — one firm to a portfolio**
Recover partner capacity at a single firm. Standardize operations across 20 acquired practices at a PE roll-up. Same product, multiplying value.

**2. Vendor-agnostic data unification**
No product connects QBO + Gusto + SharePoint + NetSuite + Sage into one queryable layer. We do. The harder it is to integrate, the wider the moat.

**3. Institutional knowledge capture**
75% of CPAs retiring. Knowledge walks out the door. The AI captures it passively as staff use it. Across a portfolio, it becomes the standardization engine.

**Threshold:** Better than asking Dave (emerging). Better than flying in consultants for 6 months (enterprise).

Margin pressure, high data density.
*Buyer: Firm owner*
