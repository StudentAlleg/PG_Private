Value Propositions

1. Operational Intelligence

One firm: recover partner capacity. PE portfolio: standardize operations across 20 acquired practices. Same product, multiplying value.

2. Vendor-Agnostic Data Unification

No product connects QBO + Gusto + SharePoint + NetSuite + Sage into one queryable layer. We do. The harder to integrate, the wider the moat.

3. Institutional Knowledge Capture

75% of CPAs retiring. Knowledge walks out the door. AI captures it passively. Across a portfolio, becomes the standardization engine.

Threshold: Emerging: better than asking Dave. Enterprise: better than flying in consultants for 6 months.
