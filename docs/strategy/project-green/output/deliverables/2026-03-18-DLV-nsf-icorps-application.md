## Application Responses

**Source of Project:** Through day-to-day work challenges

**Project Description:**
Accounting firms are losing senior partners to retirement faster than they can replace them, and the critical knowledge of how their firm operates walks out the door with them. We are building an AI-powered platform that captures a firm's processes, policies, and institutional knowledge into an intelligent assistant that any staff member can ask - so that the firm's expertise is preserved and accessible to everyone. Our founder runs his own accounting firm and built this solution from the inside, solving the exact problems he and firms like his face every day.

**Development Stage:** MVP/prototype, Validated science/underlying tech

**Project Sector:** Software
