## Strategic Constraint (R22)

GTM and public product activity are **deliberately paused** pending resolution of an employment agreement. Required sequence: (1) attorney legal opinion → (2) employer conversation → (3) resolution → (4) entity formation → (5) go to market. Product development continues privately at full speed. See `docs/strategy/risk-register.md`.
